    <div class="container mx-auto px-6">
      <h3 class="text-3xl font-bold text-center mb-10">Legal Framework & Compliance Procedures</h3>
      <div class="text-lg space-y-6">
        <p>Operating in the healthcare finance sector requires rigorous adherence to multiple regulatory frameworks and legal procedures to ensure client safety, ethical conduct, and long-term viability.</p>
        <h4 class="text-2xl font-semibold">Key Legal Frameworks:</h4>
        <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
          <li><strong>Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA):</strong> Ensures the confidentiality and security of healthcare information.</li>
          <li><strong>Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX):</strong> Mandates financial transparency and accurate record-keeping.</li>
          <li><strong>Anti-Kickback Statute & Stark Law:</strong> Prevents fraud and abuse in referrals and payments.</li>
          <li><strong>Fair Lending Practices:</strong> Ensures non-discriminatory loan processing and access to capital.</li>
          <li><strong>General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):</strong> (for EU clients) Ensures consumer privacy rights in data management.</li>
        </ul>
        <h4 class="text-2xl font-semibold">Legal Procedures Followed:</h4>
        <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
          <li>Thorough client KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) checks.</li>
          <li>Due diligence assessments before issuing loans or investment funds.</li>
          <li>Contractual review by legal and compliance officers for all financing agreements.</li>
          <li>Quarterly audits and regulatory reporting to maintain financial transparency.</li>
          <li>Compliance training for all staff and periodic third-party reviews.</li>
        </ul>
        <p>These practices ensure that all stakeholders—patients, institutions, investors, and regulators—operate within a safe, reliable, and legally protected financial ecosystem.</p>
      </div>
